# Prompt — 6 iconos de features (sección "Alles für den perfekten Spaziergang")

Una sola imagen, rejilla 2×3, para recortar 6 iconos.
Estilo de **línea** coherente con los iconos de la app (`tab-map`, `tab-services`, `tab-users`).

Orden (izq→der, fila de arriba y luego abajo):
1. Routen entdecken  2. Giftköder-Radar  3. Hunde-Services
4. Eigene Routen teilen  5. KI-Assistent  6. Community & Feed

Tras recortar, guarda en `hundezonen-assets/` como:
`icon-routen.png`, `icon-radar.png`, `icon-services.png`,
`icon-teilen.png`, `icon-ki.png`, `icon-community.png`

---

```
Create ONE image containing 6 separate flat-vector LINE icons arranged in a grid
of 2 rows x 3 columns, evenly spaced with clear empty gaps between them so each
icon can be cropped out individually. FULLY TRANSPARENT background (PNG).

STYLE (identical for all 6, this is the most important rule):
- Outline / line-art style ONLY — no solid fills, no background tiles, no circles behind
- Single stroke color: dark teal #1E6E5E, even medium-thick line weight, rounded caps and joins
- A subtle thin warm-yellow (#E8B84B) offset outline echoing each teal stroke (double-line look)
- Clean, minimal, friendly rounded shapes; LOW detail; readable at very small size
- Consistent line weight, framing and size across all 6
- Centered in each cell, generous padding, no frame, no card, no text, no numbers

THE 6 ICONS (left to right, top row then bottom row):
1. a folded map with a winding dotted route and a location pin (discover walking routes)
2. a warning triangle with an exclamation mark and a small paw print (poison-bait safety radar)
3. an open briefcase / service kit with a small paw print (dog services: vets, shops, schools)
4. a winding dotted walking route with a location pin and a small "+" circle (create & share your own route)
5. a speech/chat bubble with a tiny friendly robot face and a small sparkle (AI assistant)
6. two dog-head silhouettes side by side with a small heart (community & feed)
```

Si los junta demasiado: añade *"add wide margins and visible gaps between all 6 icons, do not let them touch"*.
Cuando esté la hoja, pásamela y las recorto y conecto a los 6 tiles.
